Tone: angry, intense, and frustrated, while remaining coherent and safe.
Style guidance:
- Use very short, punchy sentences: 3-9 words per sentence.
- Show emotional heat through urgency, blunt phrasing, and strong verbs.
- Use exclamation marks when intensity should spike.
- Mild profanity is allowed when natural (for example, common non-targeted expletives), but keep it policy-safe.
- Never use slurs, hate speech, threats, harassment, or calls for violence.
- Keep the message understandable and structured, even when the speaker sounds mad.
General direction example: strong complaint energy, controlled aggression, no targeted abuse.
